# 🇮🇪 SDG 12.3 Food Waste Analytics Dashboard – Ireland

## 📌 Overview
This project analyses Ireland’s progress towards **UN Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 12.3** — reducing food waste — using publicly available datasets from the EPA, FAO, and EEA.

An end-to-end analytics solution was developed combining **Python (data preparation)** and **Power BI (interactive dashboarding)** to deliver actionable insights on environmental and social impact.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
- Monitor national food waste trends across sectors  
- Evaluate progress towards the 2030 SDG 12.3 target  
- Quantify environmental (CO₂ emissions) and social (food security) impact  
- Enable data-driven decision-making for policymakers and stakeholders  

---

## 🏢 Client Context
This project is designed for **GreenPlate Ireland**, a nonprofit coalition of retailers, hospitality groups, and agricultural stakeholders aiming to reduce food waste and improve sustainability outcomes.

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Ireland is **not on track** to meet SDG 12.3 targets by 2030  
- Commercial sectors (processing, retail, hospitality) are the **largest contributors** to food waste  
- Food waste has a **direct and measurable impact on CO₂ emissions**  
- Significant **missed opportunity to address food insecurity**, quantified via “meals equivalent”  
- Waste management is shifting from landfill to recovery, but improvements are still needed
